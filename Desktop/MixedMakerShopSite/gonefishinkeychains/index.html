About GoneFishin Keychains

Passion for fishing meets craftsmanship

Our Story

GoneFishin Keychains started from a love of fishing and the desire to preserve those special lures that have served their time on the water. We transform retired and disabled fishing lures into functional, beautiful keychains that carry the memories of countless casts and catches.

Each vintage keychain is carefully cleaned, preserved, and mounted with quality hardware, ensuring your fishing memories last for years to come. We also create regular lure keychains from new materials and custom 3D printed items that celebrate your fishing adventures.
