What StrainSpotter does

Plant identification scanner with journal and garden features

StrainSpotter is primarily a marijuana plant identification scanner. Scan live plants or product labels to identify strains, get plant health insights, manage your garden, and track which strains work for you.

Plant identification scanner

  • Scan live marijuana plants to identify the strain
  • Scan packaged product labels for instant strain info
  • Get plant health insights and doctor features
  • Save identified strains with photos and details

Garden management

  • Track plants in your garden with photos and notes
  • Monitor plant health and growth stages
  • Organize your garden by strain, location, or status
  • Keep a complete history of each plant

Strain journal & tracking

  • Log sessions and track effects you actually felt
  • See patterns: which strains help and when
  • Mark favorites and "safe choices" for later
  • Less guessing, more confidence in what works
Why there's a price tag

Paid on purpose, so your data isn't the product

StrainSpotter is a paid app instead of "free with ads" for a reason: the whole point is that your history is private and not part of a marketing machine.

  • No ads. Nothing blinking at you while you're trying to log a session.
  • No selling your data. Your strain history isn't being packaged up for "insights".
  • No accounts required. Use it without making yet another login.
  • Supports ongoing work. The price helps pay for development, bugs, iOS updates, and new ideas.
  • Independent, single-maker app. Built by one person who actually uses the thing.

What you're paying for

Instead of chasing downloads with ads, StrainSpotter is designed like a notebook you can trust: clean, focused, and not trying to trick you into anything.

The app offers flexible payment options: you can pay once for lifetime access, or choose a membership tier that fits how you use it.
