# How to Update Your Existing MixedMakerShop Facebook Page

## Step 1: Find Your Existing Page

1. **Click the Facebook logo** (top left) to go to your home feed
2. **Click on your profile picture** (top right)
3. **Look for "Pages"** in the left sidebar menu
4. **Click "Pages"** - this will show all pages you manage
5. **Find and click on "MixedMakerShop"**

OR

1. **Use the search bar** at the top of Facebook
2. **Type "MixedMakerShop"**
3. **Click on your page** from the results

---

## Step 2: Update Category to "Gift Shop"

1. **Click "About"** tab on your page
2. **Find "Category"** section
3. **Click "Edit"** next to Category
4. **Change it to "Gift shop"** (or select from dropdown)
5. **Click "Save"**

---

## Step 3: Update About Section

1. **Stay on the "About" tab**
2. **Click "Edit"** next to "About" or "Description"
3. **Paste this Short Description:**
   ```
   A tiny studio turning ideas into things you can hold and use: 3D prints, Henry AI, StrainSpotter.app, and GoneFishin Keychains.
   ```

4. **For Long Description, paste this:**

5. **Click "Save"**

---

## Step 4: Add Website Link

1. **Still on "About" tab**
2. **Find "Contact Information"** section
3. **Click "Edit"** next to Website
4. **Add:** `https://mixedmakershop.com`
5. **Click "Save"**

---

## Step 5: Update Profile Picture

1. **Hover over your profile picture** (the circular image)
2. **Click "Update"** or the camera icon
3. **Upload your M3 logo** (170x170px recommended)
4. **Crop/adjust if needed**
5. **Click "Save"**

---

## Step 6: Update Cover Photo

1. **Hover over the cover photo area** (large banner at top)
2. **Click "Update Cover Photo"**
3. **Upload a branded cover photo** (1640x859px recommended)
   - Should include: "MixedMakerShop", tagline, website
   - Use brand colors: green #10412a, orange #f49b1a
4. **Adjust position if needed**
5. **Click "Save"**

---

## Step 7: Create Welcome Post

1. **Go to "Posts" tab** (or main page feed)
2. **Click "Create Post"**

---

## Step 8: Set Up Call-to-Action Button

1. **Look for "Add a Button"** or **"Edit Button"** on your page (usually below the cover photo)
2. **Click it**
3. **Choose:** "Contact Us" or "Learn More"
4. **Link to:** `https://mixedmakershop.com` or `https://mixedmakershop.com/contact`
5. **Click "Save"**

---

## Step 9: Create Photo Albums

1. **Click "Photos" tab**
2. **Click "Create Album"**
3. **Create these albums:**
   - **Products** - Add photos of your 3D prints, keychains, finished items
   - **Behind the Scenes** - Workspace, making process
   - **Ventures** - Henry AI, StrainSpotter, GoneFishin highlights
